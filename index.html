Things I Made

Who's Bombing Who?

whosbombingwho.coderkuttappan.com

If you wake up bleary-eyed, open your phone, and ask this every day with a sudden spike in anxiety – this is for you. A world map with conflicts marked and color-coded dots that pulse when you need to panic. Also covers the conflicts your news feed buries because they're not trendy enough. If you get offended by anything, blame Google's Gemini — it wrote the summaries, not me.

Time Since

timesince.coderkuttappan.com

How long since you called your parents? Went to the gym? Watered that plant?

If the only habit your habit tracker helped you develop is anxiety, and your to-do list reminds you of your mean 7th grade teacher – this is for you. A quiet counter you install once and forget about. Put in when you did the thing, and see how long it's been. You decide what to do with that information – no pressure, no notifications.

Paste Clean

pasteclean.coderkuttappan.com

If you've wondered why you can't copy-paste from Word to Outlook in 2026, in the age of AI, without all hell breaking loose – this is for you. Word, Gmail, Outlook, Excel, WhatsApp — whatever it is, copy what you need to, paste into the format you need, then send it on its way.
